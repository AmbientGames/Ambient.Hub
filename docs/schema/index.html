About

Schema is a single-player voxel game that blends creative building with RPG adventure. Generate worlds from real-world height maps or procedurally, then explore, mine, craft, build machines, and follow saga-driven stories — all offline, at your own pace.

Features

World Generation

Create worlds from real elevation data or procedural generation. Import height maps from anywhere on Earth and play in them.

Building & Crafting

Mine resources, smelt ores in furnaces, and build anything from simple shelters to complex machines.

RPG & Sagas

Encounter characters with dialogue trees, follow branching story arcs, trade, and explore narrative-driven adventures.

Climate & Simulation

Worlds come alive with dynamic weather, soil saturation, gravity, heat diffusion, and living vegetation.

XML Modding

Customize nearly every aspect of the game through XML. Define blocks, substances, equipment, consumables, characters, dialogue, quests, and saga arcs — no code required.
